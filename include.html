Background

Currently, I am the Director of Design for Include, a non-profit organization at the University of California Davis that helps build websites for local businesses in the area.

Summary

As the Director of Design, I’ve learned and improved many skills such as leadership, teamwork, communication, organization, and decision making.

My responsiblities include: leading the design team, creating lesson plans and marketing material, and leading design workshops.
